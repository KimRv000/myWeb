MusiClub

MusiClub is a social network designed to connect people through their music taste, it was created from user stories and from high and low fidelity prototypes. It is a responsive Single-page Application (SPA) in which a router was implemented for navigation between the different views of the application using Firebase as an external service to manage application data. The website has a home screen that it gives you the option to get registered or to log in, in both options you can link your Google account to access. Once the session has started you will find the feed with a section where the user's email will appear to identify yourself at the top of the page, you will find a container where you can write a post and below you will be able to see the different publications created with the respective email of their author to identify it. You will be able to like and unlike posts and you will also be able to edit and delete your posts.
